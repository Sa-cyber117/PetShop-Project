About Us

Super Pets

At p3tZ, we're more than just a pet store - we're a passionate community dedicated to the health, happiness, and well-being of pets and their owners. Since 2024, we've been proud to serve the Gotham City area with a wide range of premium pet products, expert advice, and personalized service.

Read More

Our Mission

Our mission is simple, to provide pet owners with everything they need to give their furry, feathered, or scaled companions the best possible care. We're here to support pet owners at every step of their journey.

Our History

p3tZ was born out of a love for animals and a desire to create a welcoming space where pet owners could find everything they need under one roof.

Our Team

Behind p3tZ is a team of dedicated individuals who share a common love for animals and a commitment to excellence. Everyone at p3tZ is here to ensure that you and your pet receive the highest level of care and attention.

Community Involvement

We believe in giving back to the community that has supported us over the years. That's why we're proud to sponsor local pet adoption events, partner with animal shelters and rescue organizations, and host educational workshops for pet owners.
